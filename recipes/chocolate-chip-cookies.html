Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Thin and crisp chocolate chip cookies. My go to recipe.

  • 1/2 lb unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 c light brown sugar
  • 1/2 c white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 c all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 c chocolate chips
  • 1 c toasted walnuts chopped

Steps:

  1. beat butter and sugars in mixer until light
  2. add eggs one at a time, beat to mix between each addition
  3. add vanilla and salt
  4. sift in flour and baking soda, mix in briefly
  5. add chocolate chips and walnuts
  6. line cookie sheets with parchment paper and bake at 400F for 10 minutes
