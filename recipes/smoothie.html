Smoothie

Description:

Smoothie I drink most weekdays

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup european yogurt
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon powdered instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon Udo's oil
  • 1 banana
  • 1 handful of frozen strawberries
  • 1 ice cube

Steps:

  1. Add milk, yogurt, protein powder, instant coffee granules, Udo's oil and banana to blender. Briefly blend.
  2. Add frozen srawberries and ice cube to blender. Blend until mostly smooth
